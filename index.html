About Us

Ceamara was created back in 2005 by John Connolly. After completing his photography degree he discovered his passion of teaching. After over 10 years of experience with freelance work all over the world, John came up with the idea of offering photography lessons with the aim of introducing people to the art of photography as well as sharing his knowledge with photographers of all skill levels.


Now, with over 20 years of experience he runs Ceamara with his team of talented photographers.
